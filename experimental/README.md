# Traccia Experimental Modules

These modules are maintained for research and future use but are NOT part of the core SDK.

- **sdk.py**: ResidualLoop wrapper (closed-loop agent behavior system)
- **residual/**: 4D execution quality scoring
- **entropy/**: Controlled adversarial testing
- **loop/**: Closed-loop feedback training
- **behavior/**: Cross-runtime behavior analysis

They may be activated in future enterprise or research releases.
